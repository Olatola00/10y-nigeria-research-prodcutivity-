The Disciplinary Landscape

An analysis of research output across major academic disciplines, highlighting areas of strength and opportunities for diversification.

Overall Disciplinary Distribution

Medical & Health Sciences dominate the research landscape, consistent with global trends where applied sciences addressing societal needs receive more funding. The low output from Humanities and the Arts suggests a need for diversified research priorities and funding.

Disciplinary Productivity Over Time

This chart shows the evolving "pairwise contest" between disciplines. While all fields show growth, Medical & Health Sciences and Natural Sciences vie for the top spots, highlighting dynamic shifts in research focus over the decade.
