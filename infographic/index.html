The Powerhouses: Who Leads the Research?

The top 10 institutions account for a staggering 62% of the nation's entire research output, highlighting a major concentration of productivity.

Federal universities dominate, but the rise of private institutions like Covenant University signals a dynamic shift in Nigeria's academic landscape.

What's Being Researched? The Disciplinary Divide

Applied Sciences Dominate

Medical & Health Sciences lead significantly, reflecting a focus on addressing immediate societal needs. In contrast, the Humanities and Arts receive the least research attention, pointing to an imbalance in funding and priorities.
